Bank Details

We don't keep a record of your bank details ourselves - they are kept safely with GoCardless who handle our billing for the Fruit and Veg scheme.

Changing your bank details

If you need to change your bank details, the fastest way to change them is to contact GoCardless directly - you can reach them on 020 7183 8674, or email them at help@gocardless.com.

For more information please see their support site, help.gocardless.com.

If you have any other queries you can always reach us at vegscheme@growingcommunities.org or call us during office hours on 020 7502 7588.
